How We Serve the Community

Faith without works is dead. We demonstrate God's love through practical acts of compassion.

Feeding Program

Providing nutritious meals to hungry families in our community every week.

Every Saturday, our team prepares and distributes food packages to vulnerable families. No one should go hungry while we can help.

Support This Program

Orphan Support

Providing care, education, and love to orphaned children in our community.

We partner with families to provide a loving home, school fees, uniforms, and emotional support to orphaned and vulnerable children.

Widow Support

Standing with widows, providing practical help and spiritual support.

The Bible commands us to care for widows (James 1:27). We provide food, prayer, counseling, and financial assistance to widows in need.

Taking the Gospel to unreached villages and communities.

Regular outreach missions to villages with no established church — preaching the Gospel, praying for the sick, and establishing prayer groups.
