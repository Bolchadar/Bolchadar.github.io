Featured Message

Walking in God's Purpose — You Were Created for More

  "For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do." — Ephesians 2:10

You are not an accident. You are not a mistake. Before the foundation of the world, God had a plan and a purpose specifically for you. The verse today reminds us that we are His masterpiece — crafted with intention, love, and divine design.

Many people spend their lives wondering why they are here. They search for meaning in careers, relationships, and achievements. But the truth of God's Word declares something greater: you were made to walk in the works that God prepared for you before time began.

This means that no matter what you have been through — the failures, the losses, the disappointments — God has not abandoned His purpose for your life. He is still working, still weaving, still preparing the path that leads to your divine destiny.

Today, be encouraged. Step boldly into what God has placed in your heart. Trust His timing. Walk in faith. Your best days are not behind you — they are ahead, in the hands of the One who created you for His glory.

Today's Prayer Points

Thank God for creating you with purpose and intentionality.
Ask God to reveal the specific works He has prepared for you.
Pray for boldness to step into your God-given calling, despite fear or doubt.
Intercede for someone else who needs to discover their purpose in God.
