About Apostle MJ Ministries

Rooted in faith, driven by love, committed to the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Meet the Apostle

Apostle MJ

Apostle MJ is a Spirit-filled servant of God who has dedicated his life to the Great Commission — preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, healing the sick, setting the captives free, and discipling nations.

Called into ministry by the grace of God, Apostle MJ has served communities across the region with passion and humility. His ministry is marked by signs and wonders, transformed lives, and a deep love for God's people.

With a heart for the poor and the lost, Apostle MJ has established charitable programs that support orphans, widows, and vulnerable families — bringing tangible evidence of God's love to those who need it most.

His teaching ministry is rooted in the Word of God, delivered with clarity, conviction, and compassion. Thousands of souls have been saved and thousands of lives transformed through this ministry.

Our Journey

Ministry History

The Beginning
Called by God
Apostle MJ received the divine call to ministry. He began preaching in small gatherings and home churches, with just a handful of believers who caught the vision.
Early Days
First Church Established
The ministry officially established its first church congregation. The first Bible study groups were formed and the prayer ministry was launched.
Growing
Charity Work Begins
A heart for the community led to the launch of feeding programs, orphan support, and widow care ministries that continue to this day.
Expansion
Media Ministry Launched
The ministry went online — Facebook Live services, YouTube channel, and TikTok outreach extended our reach far beyond the local congregation.
Today
Growing Stronger
With hundreds of active members, an online community, and ongoing charity work, the ministry continues to expand and touch lives for God's glory.
What We Believe

Statement of Faith

Our beliefs are founded on the eternal, infallible Word of God — the Holy Bible.

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

The Bible

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God and the final rule for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone — His death, burial, and resurrection — and is a free gift of God's grace.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, including the gifts of the Spirit as described in 1 Corinthians 12.

Water Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion as an outward declaration of an inward faith in Jesus Christ.

Divine Healing

We believe in healing for the body as provided for in the atonement of Jesus Christ (Isaiah 53:5).

The Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible, and imminent return of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Eternal Life

We believe in the resurrection of the dead — eternal life for the saved and eternal separation for the lost.
